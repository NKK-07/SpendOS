# Core Philosophy

## Principle 1: Simplicity First

The simplest architecture that satisfies requirements is preferred.

Complexity requires explicit justification.

---

## Principle 2: Reliability Before Scale

A system that scales but frequently fails is considered a failed design.

Reliability is a prerequisite for scale.

---

## Principle 3: Evolution Over Rewrites

Architectures should evolve incrementally.

Avoid solutions that require future rewrites.

---

## Principle 4: Architecture Must Match Organization

Architecture should reflect:

* Team size
* Engineering maturity
* Operational capabilities
* Hiring plans

Avoid architectures that exceed organizational capacity.
